ABOUT

We are a Boston-based group that plays a blend of funk, rock, jazz, and R&B. We draw inspiration from a range of artists, both old and new, and perform original music and covers. The group consists of the Orlando cousins on vocals/sax (Joe) and keyboards (Dom), Josh Soper on guitar, John Krone on bass, and Nick Adair on drums. Outside of music, we are a medley of engineers and analysts that enjoy RuneScape, chess, and occasionally skiing together :)
